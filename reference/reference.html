03

House rules

Always

  • Fraunces for headings & the sender’s message; Schibsted Grotesk for UI
  • Paper background, white cards, 1px hairline borders
  • One accent (vermillion) — under 5% of any screen
  • Serial numbers, stamps, parcel tags, perforated dividers
  • Tabular numerals wherever money or counts appear
  • One moment of celebration: the redemption confirm

Never

  • Inter, gradients, glassmorphism, purple
  • rounded-2xl cards floating on drop shadows
  • Emoji in headings or UI chrome
  • Icon-in-circle feature grids and mascot empty-states
  • Pie charts, dual axes, rainbow series
  • Untouched shadcn defaults shipped as “design”
